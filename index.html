Blood Pressure & Diet

High sodium foods can raise blood pressure, while potassium-rich foods like spinach, bananas, and salmon help lower it. The DASH diet emphasizes fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins to maintain healthy blood pressure.

  • Limit sodium to under 2,300mg daily
  • Increase potassium intake with leafy greens
  • Choose lean proteins like fish and poultry

Blood Sugar Management

Complex carbohydrates and fiber help stabilize blood sugar levels, while simple sugars cause spikes. Pairing proteins with carbs slows glucose absorption and maintains steady energy levels.

  • Choose whole grains over refined carbs
  • Include protein with each meal
  • Eat fiber-rich vegetables and legumes

Oxygen & Circulation

Iron-rich foods support hemoglobin production for oxygen transport, while antioxidants improve circulation. Nitrate-rich vegetables like beets enhance oxygen delivery to muscles and organs.

  • Eat iron-rich foods like spinach and lean meats
  • Include vitamin C to enhance iron absorption
  • Try beetroot juice for improved circulation
